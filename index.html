The Rappers

Rappers in the USA are the heartbeat of hip-hop culture, using rhythm and rhymes to tell stories of
triumph, struggle, and identity. Originating from the streets of New York in the 1970s, rap has evolved
into a global phenomenon, shaping music, fashion, and activism. Icons like Tupac Shakur and
The Notorious B.I.G. laid the foundation with their vivid storytelling and raw emotion. Today, artists like
Kendrick Lamar, Nicki Minaj, and Drake continue to push boundaries, blending personal narratives
with cultural commentary. Each rapper, from underground talents to chart-topping superstars, adds
a unique voice to this ever-evolving art form.
